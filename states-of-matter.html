How It Works

Drag the slider to heat or cool. Particles interact through intermolecular forces: solid at low temperatures, liquid between the melting and boiling points, and gas above the boiling point.

About Phase Changes

During a phase change, temperature stays constant while energy is absorbed or released — this is latent heat, the energy needed to overcome or form intermolecular bonds.
